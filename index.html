RL Snake Player

Applied reinforcement learning to the 2D grid-based game called Snake, including training algorithms of Q-learning with linear function approximation, Deep Q-Network (DQN), and Double Deep Q-Network (DDQN) with experience replay to observe how these techniques compared in performance on a partially observable Markov decision process (POMDP) of the traditional version of the game. Final Project for CS 394R (Graduate Reinforcement Learning) with partner.
